Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot password" at the login screen and follow the instructions in your email.

Where can I view my purchase history?

Go to your account dashboard and click on "Purchase History" in the menu.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel at any time from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing cycle.
